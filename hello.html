Did you know?

  • In the wild, Bald Eagles will lay 1 to 3 eggs in a clutch. In rare cases they will lay 4.
  • Bald Eagles have no sense of smell. They can taste though, and won't eat something that tastes spoiled
  • Bald eagles can live up to 28 years in the wild and 36 years in captivity.
  • Bald eagle eggs incubate for 34 to 36 days
